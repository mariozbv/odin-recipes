Pasta Carbonara

This bacon carbonara is a super rich, classic "bacon and egg" spaghetti dish that's great to serve for company. This recipe also makes an unusual brunch offering.

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound spaghetti
  2. 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided, or as needed
  3. 8 slices bacon, diced
  4. 1 onion, chopped
  5. 1 clove garlic, minced
  6. ¼ cup dry white wine
  7. 4 large eggs, beaten
  8. ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  9. salt and black pepper to taste
  10. 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  11. 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
